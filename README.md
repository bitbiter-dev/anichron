# Anichron

A self-hosted media flashback system. Crawls your NAS and surfaces your photos and videos as an Instagram Story-style "On This Day" experience — entirely under your control.

## Highlights

- Daily flashbacks grouped by year, navigated as a story (tap / swipe / hold to pause)
- Live Photo support, burst grouping, and blurhash loading placeholders
- Star, like, or hide memories to personalise future flashbacks
- Monthly, seasonal, and yearly recap views
- Daily email notifications with a deep link to today's memories
- Multi-user — each person keeps their own interaction state; family libraries supported
- GPU-accelerated transcoding (Intel QuickSync → NVENC → AMF → software fallback)
- No cloud dependency — your NAS, your data — [AGPL-3.0](LICENSE)
